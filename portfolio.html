Asset Tracking System

Developed a tracking system for automobiles in the assembly plant. The web-based application used QR codes to track the location of assets that would ultimately create a fully functional automobile. The asset (or parts in this case) will be tracked as they movie through assembly lines in the manufacturing plant when their QR code is scanned at every station. There were two modules for this application. Engineer module allowed the technicians at every assembly line to scan the asset before initiating manufacturing. This would keep the application updated with latest tracking information for the admins as well as exact longitude and latitude of the asset. The Admin module allowed the plant managers to oversee the asset operation as it moved through the manufacturing facility to easily track and issue instruction to line technicians. This project provided enhanced efficiency of the plant and decreased the overall cost of reworking an asset or the cost incurred by scraped parts.
